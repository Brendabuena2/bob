## What is BOB?

BOB combines the power of Bitcoin security and stability with innovation enabled by the EVM and the OP stack. BOB supports the Bitcoin ecosystem incl. Ordinals, Lightning and Nostr, powered by cross-chain light clients, a universal Bitcoin smart contract SDK and the RiscZero zkVM.

Our mission is to create a Bitcoin rollup ecosystem to power innovation and experimentation without relying on Bitcoin forks.

## Values

- **Experimentation**: We enable a diverse space of innovators to play with ordinals, ZK, SocialFi, DAOs, and more.
- **Real-world impact**: Innovations improve people's lives by giving them access to Bitcoin values.
- **Freedom of choice**: Builders focus on the unique values of their projects without having to re-invent the wheel.
